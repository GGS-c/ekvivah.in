Our Journey

The First Meeting (2018)

Our story began serendipitously during a family function. Arjun's quick wit and Riya's infectious laughter created an instant connection that went beyond formal introductions. What started as stolen glances turned into late-night conversations and the realization that we shared a common vision for life.

The Forever Ask (2024)

Arjun planned a beautiful surprise at a family retreat in the mountains. Surrounded by twinkling lights and the blessings of the night sky, he asked Riya to be his partner forever. It was a moment steeped in tradition, faith, and overwhelming joy, sealing our commitment to a lifelong journey together.
